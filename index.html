About Dr. Koch

Benjamin Koch works across macroeconomics, psychology, and international law. His research and writing examine how legal architectures, institutional design, and human behaviour interact to shape resilience, growth, and stability in complex systems.

Current work ranges from transatlantic macro-financial coordination and telecom-sector architectures to psychological resilience frameworks and structured approaches to mental clarity in the digital age.

Views expressed in publications and media appear in a strictly personal capacity and do not represent any institution.
