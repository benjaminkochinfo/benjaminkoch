About this work

What this space is – and what it is not

This is a calm, structured online space for people who carry a lot – in their heads, on their calendar, or in their relationships. The focus is on clarity, resilience and long-range thinking rather than diagnosis or pathology.

You might be a professional in a demanding environment, a young parent balancing roles, a student facing choices, or simply someone in a life transition. The common thread is that you want to see more clearly and act more deliberately.

This is not psychotherapy, not crisis intervention, and not a substitute for medical or psychiatric treatment. If you are in acute distress or at risk of self-harm, please contact local emergency services or a crisis hotline in your country.

Services

  • Decisions with long-term consequences (career, partnership, place to live)

Structure of the work

Frame

Practical frame & boundaries

What this is not
  • Not psychotherapy or medical treatment
  • Not crisis intervention or emergency support
  • Not a substitute for psychiatric or medical care
  • Not legal, tax, or financial advice
For whom this is suitable
  • People who can reflect and take responsibility for their own actions
  • People who want to understand patterns, not just fix symptoms
  • People who are willing to commit to changes in habits or structures
  • People who value calm, precise, and honest conversations
If you are in acute crisis

If you are in acute psychological crisis, at risk of harming yourself or others, or experiencing severe symptoms (e.g. psychosis, severe depression, suicidal thoughts), please contact local emergency services, a crisis hotline, or a licensed mental health professional in your country.

This online consulting space cannot provide emergency care or crisis intervention and does not replace local, in-person support structures.
